## 🎯 Scoring System

- **Easy Problems**: 10 points
- **Medium Problems**: 20 points
- **Hard Problems**: 30 points
- **Streak Bonus**: +5 points per streak level

Example: Solving a Hard problem on a 3-streak = 30 + (3 × 5) = 45 points!
